When should I get my tan?
When should I get my tan?

Newbies, to ease your wondering mind I recommend doing a trial run a few weeks prior to your event so you can meet me, become familiar with the spray tan process, and see the results before your eyes. For my returning clients, it is recommended that you get your tan 1-2 days prior to your event.

Is there anything I need to do before my appointment?
Is there anything I need to do before my appointment?

One word: Exfoliate! Exfoliating 1-2 times the day before your appointment hits the reset button on your skin’s natural shedding cycle. The harder you scrub, the better the results! For more tips on before and after care, please refer to the Care Instructions page.

*Please be sure to read the Care Instructions page prior to your appointment.

Is everything I need provided for me?
Is everything I need provided for me?

Absolutely! I sincerely hope that your spray tan appointment with me is something that you look forward to, not stress about! I come prepared with my spray tan kit which includes everything you may need during your appointment. All I ask is that you try to relax, and enjoy the process!

What do I wear during my spray tan? After?
What do I wear during my spray tan? After?

For my women, whatever you leave on or take off is completely up to you! I have seen it all, I just want you to feel comfortable. With that being said, don’t forget to consider tan lines. If you are breastfeeding, consider wearing nipple covers.

For my men, I ask that genitals remain covered during the entire duration of your appointment. Acceptable attire may include boxers, briefs, swimming trunks, your pretty-pink thong, or whatever floats your boat! Feel free to message me with any specific requests.

Does the spray tan stain or smell?
Does the spray tan stain or smell?

I always recommend you wear dark, loose-fitted clothing after your spray tan appointment. However, modern-day spray tans have a lot to offer, including a fast-drying capability resulting in little to no transfer. If any transfer does occur, no problem! Just toss your clothes in the wash. It doesn’t stain nails or hair, either!

My solutions do not have any scents.

Do spray tans act as sunscreen?
Do spray tans act as sunscreen?

Let’s bust this myth right here, right now! Your spray tan will only act as an extra layer of skin, so layer up! SPF and a stylish hat are always my first things to pack.

Is it safe to get a spray tan while pregnant or breastfeeding?
Is it safe to get a spray tan while pregnant or breastfeeding?

It is safe to get sprayed while pregnant, however, I always recommend consulting with a doctor prior to getting sprayed. If you are breastfeeding, I suggest wearing pasties or nipple covers during your appointment, but a simple wipe to remove the tan from the area afterward does the trick.
